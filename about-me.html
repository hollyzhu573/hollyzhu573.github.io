HELLO, MY NAME IS HOLLY

WELCOME TO MY PERSONAL PAGE!

i'm excited to share my journey with you all. i am currently a second year at the University of California, San Diego, studying computer science and minoring in design. i began this journey inspired by my software engineer parents and a passion for art and design. i'm excited to see what the future holds!

besides the technical stuff:

i love figure skating! i've been skating for almost fourteen years and counting. covid-19 cut my competitive career short, but i now skate for fun here on UCSD's club team and partake in intercollegiate competitions as well!

i'm learning calligraphy and like to make/edit videos. way back in 2016, i created an instagram account to display the random bits of art and hand lettering i had been trying out. that soon turned into a creative place to post my artistics endeavors, from sticker-making to bullet journaling and graphic art.

that was how holligraphy was formed.

i ended up branching out to youtube videos as well, and used holligraphy as a creative outlet where i could experiment with different things. though it is less active now due to school, it is a passion project that is dear to my heart.
